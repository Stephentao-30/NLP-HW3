EE C128. Feedback Control Systems
Catalog Description:  Analysis and synthesis of linear feedback control systems in transform and time domains. Control system design by root locus, frequency response, and state space methods. Applications to electro-mechanical and mechatronics systems.
Units:  4
Also Offered As:  MEC ENG C134
Related Areas:
Control, Intelligent Systems, and Robotics (CIR)
Prerequisites:  EECS 16A or MEC ENG 100; MEC ENG 132 or EL ENG 120.
Formats:  Fall: 3.0 hours of lecture, 1.0 hours of discussion, and 3.0 hours of laboratory per week
Spring: 3.0 hours of lecture, 1.0 hours of discussion, and 3.0 hours of laboratory per week
Grading Basis:  letter
Final Exam Status:  Written final exam conducted during the scheduled final exam period
