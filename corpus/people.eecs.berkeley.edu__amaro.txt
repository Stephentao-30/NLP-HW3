Emmanuel Amaro
I'm a Computer Science PhD student at UC Berkeley. I work at the  NetSys lab , where I am advised by  Scott Shenker . I am broadly interested in systems, networking, and computer architecture. Before UC Berkeley, I received my Masters degree at Georgia Tech where I was advised by  Hadi Esmaeilzadeh .
