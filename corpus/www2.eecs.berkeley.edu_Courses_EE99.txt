EE 99. Individual Study and Research for Undergraduates
Catalog Description:  Supervised independent study and research for students with fewer than 60 units completed.
Units:  1.0-4.0
Prerequisites:  Freshman or sophomore standing and consent of instructor. Minimum GPA of 3.4 required.
Formats:  Fall: 1.0-4.0 hours of independent study per week
Spring: 1.0-4.0 hours of independent study per week
Summer: 1.0-5.0 hours of independent study per week
Grading basis:  passFail
Final exam status:  No final exam
Also listed as:  EL ENG 99
