Salto the wall-jumping robot is the most vertically agile ever built - EECS at Berkeley
EECS Prof. Ronald Fearing,  EECS PhD student Justin Yim, post doc Dr.  Mark Plecnik, and  ME PhD student Duncan Haldane  have created Salto, the most v ertically agile jumping robot.   Salto can repeatedly jump 1 meter  vertically at almost two times per second.   Salto is featured in the premier issue  of  Science Robotics  (Dec. 6).
