I am an Assistant Professor in the  Robotics Institute  and  School of Computer Science  at  Carnegie Mellon University .
I lead the  Interactive and Trustworthy Robotics Lab (Intent Lab) . My research interests lie in the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and AI safety.
I obtained my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences (EECS) from  University of California Berkeley  in 2022. I received a B.S. in Computer Science from the  University of Maryland College Park  in 2016. Before joining CMU in Sept. 2023, I was a postdoctoral scholar at UC Berkeley and spent spring 2021 working at NVIDIA in the  Autonomous Vehicle Research Group .
