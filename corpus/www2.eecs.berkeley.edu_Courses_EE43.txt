EE 43. Introductory Electronics Laboratory
Catalog Description:  Using and understanding electronics laboratory equipment such as oscilloscope, power supplies, function generator, multimeter, curve-tracer, and RLC-meter. Includes a term project of constructing and testing a robot or other appropriate electromechanical device.
Units:  1.0
Prerequisites:  42 (may be taken concurrently) or equivalent or consent of instructor.
