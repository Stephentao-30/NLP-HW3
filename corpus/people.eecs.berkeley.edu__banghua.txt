About me
I received the B.S. degree in electronic engineering from Tsinghua University. I'm currently working as a second year Ph.D. student, advised by Prof. Jiantao Jiao and Prof. Michael I. Jordan. I was also very fortunate to work with Prof. Jacob Steinhardt and Prof. David Tse.
Research
My research interests are mainly on the fundamental problems in statistics, machine learning, mechanism design and security. In particular, I would like to deepen the understanding of the fundamental limit in robust statistics, distributed statistical inference, mechanism design and cryptocurrency.
