EECS Safety Manual
Faculty, staff, and students are encouraged to return to their offices, labs, and other research spaces without occupancy or distancing restrictions.  Everyone is responsible for staying current on the latest
campus safety guidelines
.
Cory and Soda Building Access Requirements
Complete the
Workplace Safety Training
program through the
UC Learning Center
(estimated time: 30 minutes).
If you are unable to login to the Workplace Safety Training, please review the
Workplace Safety Training PDF version
and proceed to Step 2.
Review the
Building Emergency Plans
for Cory or Soda Hall to learn emergency evacuation routes and procedures.
Review the
EECS Department Safety Contacts
(below) so you know whom to contact before an actual emergency.
Submit an
EECS Safety Compliance Form
to confirm that you have completed the above steps.
If you need card key access or a metal door key, submit an
EECS Card Key/Metal Key Request Form
.
You must complete steps 1-4 above before submitting this request.
After completing the EECS Card Key/Metal Key Request Form, if you need to pick up a physical door key (in addition to card key activation) for Cory or Soda, schedule a time for an
EECS Metal Key Pick-Up
.
If you have applied for access and your card key isn’t working, submit an
EECS Cardkey Access Problems Form
.
Questions?
keys@eecs.berkeley.edu
