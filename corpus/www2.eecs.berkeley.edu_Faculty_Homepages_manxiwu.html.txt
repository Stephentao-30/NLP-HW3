Biography
Dr. Manxi Wu received her Ph.D. in social and engineering systems from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2021. She is currently a postdoctoral researcher in our department. Her areas of interest are game theory, multi-agent learning, mechanism design, and societal networks. She will be teaching EE 290 (Design of Society Scale Systems) in Spring 2022.
