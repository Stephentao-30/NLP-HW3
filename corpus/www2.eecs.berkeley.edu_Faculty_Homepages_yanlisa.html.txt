Biography
Lisa Yan is an Assistant Teaching Professor in. Her current research interests are data analysis of student learning in large computer science and data science courses, computing ethics education, and teaching assistant education. She received the 2024 Faculty Award for Outstanding Mentorship of GSIs and is the designer and instructor of Data 6 (Introduction to Computational Thinking with Data and Society) and Data 375, a professional pedagogy course for Data Science GSIs. She received her MS and PhD in Electrical Engineering from Stanford University.
