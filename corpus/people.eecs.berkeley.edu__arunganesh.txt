Bio
I am a second-year graduate student in the  Theory Group  at UC Berkeley, where I am fortunate to be advised by  Satish Rao . I am currently working on projects in algorithms under uncertainty, approximation algorithms, and phylogenetic reconstruction. Prior to joining Berkeley, I got my BS in Computer Science at Duke University, where I had the pleasure of working with  Debmalya Panigrahi .
For Summer 2019 I am interning at  Google Brain  with  Kunal Talwar .
