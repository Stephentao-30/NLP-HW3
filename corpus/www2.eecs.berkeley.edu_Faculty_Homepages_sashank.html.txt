Biography
Sashank Krishnamurthy is teaching EE 140/240A in Spring 2024. He received his Ph.D. from our department in 2020. Since then, he has been employed as a research scientist at Intel Labs. His research areas are chip-to-chip photonic links, design of high-speed, energy-efficient electrical and optical transceivers, and design of high-speed, low-jitter clock generation and distribution.
