The EECS Newsletter is a repository for articles and stories about the people, research, and history of the  Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences  at UC Berkeley.
If you would like to support Berkeley EECS in our mission to educate future leaders, create dynamic and collaborative research environments, and serve our local and global communities, please consider  making a gift .
Also, if you want to suggest a Berkeley EECS student, one of our alumni, or a faculty member to profile; or you know of a research topic or news story that deserves a write-up, please let us know by sending mail to  news@eecs.berkeley.edu
