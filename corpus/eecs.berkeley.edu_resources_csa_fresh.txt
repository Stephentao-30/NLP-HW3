Center for Student Affairs
Center for Student Affairs
The EECS Center for Student Affairs (CSA) provides individual academic and administrative assistance to departmental visitors, students (both graduate and undergraduate), faculty and staff.
Meet Our Staff
Our goal is to provide friendly, accurate and efficient academic and administrative services to everyone. Our team is here to assist you in your search for admission information, financial support, research and teaching appointments. We also handle issues related to student diversity, retention and academic success. Please do not hesitate to contact us with any questions you may have about our student programs.
