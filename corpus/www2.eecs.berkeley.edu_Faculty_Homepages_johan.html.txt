Biography
Johan Vanderhaegen will be teaching EE 240C in Spring 2023. He received his Ph.D. from our department in 2005. He is currently a hardware engineer for Verily (Google Life Sciences). His area of interest is the design of high-precision, low-power analog integrated circuits, with an emphasis on switched capacitor circuits and analog-to-digital converters.
