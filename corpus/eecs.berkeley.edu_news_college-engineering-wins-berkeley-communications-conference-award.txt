College of Engineering wins Berkeley Communications Conference award - EECS at Berkeley
The College of Engineering Marketing & Communications team have won a Berkeley Communications Conference (BC2) Collateral Award for their “Berkeley Engineering bear (BEBear)” campaign.  To celebrate the holiday season of Berkeley Engineering’s sesquicentennial in 2018, the team created a template for an origami bear that could be printed out, folded, posed, photographed, and shared with the campus community on social media.  The winning team included Adam Lau, Linda Vu, Sarah Yang, Julianna Fleming and Steve McConnell.  BC2 is an annual event for campus communications professionals.
