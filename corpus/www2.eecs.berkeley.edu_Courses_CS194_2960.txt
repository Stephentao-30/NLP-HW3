CS 194-138. Cyberwar
Catalog Description:  Topics will vary semester to semester. See the Computer Science Division announcements.

Class homepage on inst.eecs
General Catalog listing
Department Notes:  The Cyberwar class will give students a chance to use
the knowledge that they have gained in other CS courses to real-world
scenarios.  Students will examine real running applications for
vulnerabilities, and report them back to the vendor; and will also
forensically examine real cyberwar attacks, and uncover their secrets.  An
FAQ on the Fall 2017 offering is available at  https://people.eecs.berkeley.edu/~tygar/cyberwarF17faq.html .
This section of 94/294 is only offered for 4 units.
