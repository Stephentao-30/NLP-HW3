The MAS-IC Program Has Been Discontinued
The Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences department has decided to discontinue the department’s online program, “Master of Advanced Study in Integrated Circuits”.  The program began in 2012 and the last cohort was admitted in spring 2017.  The program will remain active until all current students have been given the opportunity to complete the degree.  However, no new students will be admitted.
Current MAS-IC students can find information in the  MAS-IC Student Guide
Further questions of the program should be directed to the EECS Graduate Admissions Office at gradadmissions@eecs.berkeley.edu.
