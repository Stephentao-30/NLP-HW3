Ion Stoica wins ACM SIGOPS Mark Weiser Award - EECS at Berkeley
Ion Stoica has won the ACM SIGOPS Mark Weiser Award. Created in 2001 by ACM SIGOPS, t he award is named in honor of Mark Weiser, a computing visionary recognized for his research accomplishments during his career at Xerox PARC.  Recipients are selected for “contributions that are highly creative, innovative, and possibly high-risk, in keeping with the visionary spirit of Mark Weiser.”
