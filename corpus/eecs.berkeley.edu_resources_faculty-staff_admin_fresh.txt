Room Access
Students are automatically given access to buildings, rooms and labs based on their course enrollment.
If you have problems with your access, email keys@eecs.berkeley.edu or visit 387 Soda Hall M-F
8:00am-11:30am
or
1:00pm-3:30pm
.
Lost and Found
Take found items to 387 Soda Hall or 253 Cory Hall, where they will be kept for 4 weeks. After the fourth week, they will be taken to the campus lost and found office, if appropriate. You can see what type of items are accepted by the campus lost and found office, and search their database,
here
. All items not accepted by the campus lost and found office will be donated.
Mail
Internal campus mail and outgoing mail using department and research barcode stickers should be taken to 253 Cory Hall or 387 Soda Hall. If you missed the pickup, Campus Mail Services has several
late boxes
(cleared by 2 pm).
Use US Postal Service mailboxes for personal mail. The closest is at the corner of Hearst and Euclid.
Mail sent to Cory Hall and Soda Hall requires the following mail codes:
Cory Hall: 1770
Soda Hall: 1776
Faculty and staff mailboxes are located at the Cory and Soda Hall Front Offices.
Graduate mailboxes are available for EE & CS students at the Cory and Soda Hall Front Offices. For more information see:
New Grads: Cory and Soda Halls
.
Shipping and Receiving
All shipping and receiving using department and research funds, including FedEx and UPS, is handled by ERSO Shipping and Receiving in 266 Sutardja Dai Hall, email erso-receiving@erso.berkeley.edu
If you are expecting a package, there will be a 1-day delay from the time your courier says they have delivered your item. Campus services receives the package and delivers it to its final destination by the next business day.
For personal FedEx and UPS shipments:
The nearest FedEx box is in the lobby of Etcheverry Hall, via the entrance near the Hearst Ave end of the breezeway between Etcheverry and Soda. There is another on the Campanile side of Evans Hall.
There is a UPS box at the corner of Euclid and Hearst.
ERSO Shipping & Receiving resources
Available Equipment
Fax machine and thermal laminator available for faculty/staff use at the Cory Hall Front Office.
The following equipment can be borrowed by faculty and staff:
Hand carts
Projectors
Conference calling devices
Poster boards and easels.
Equipment and Battery Disposal
To dispose of large equipment, email shipping and receiving at erso-receiving@erso.berkeley.edu
The Cory Hall and Soda Hall Front Offices have small battery recycling bins available for staff/faculty use.
If you have a large amount of batteries to dispose of, contact Berkeley EH&S and provide a chart string in order to request a pick up and/or view other
drop off locations
. For more information on hazardous waste disposal visit
Berkeley EH&S
.
