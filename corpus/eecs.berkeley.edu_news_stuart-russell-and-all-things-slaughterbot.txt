Stuart Russell and all things "Slaughterbot" - EECS at Berkeley
CS Prof. Stuart Russell is featured in an interview by the “Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists” titled  “ As much death as you want”: UC Berkeley’s Stuart Russell on “Slaughterbots. ”   Russell discusses the genesis of the hit YouTube video, the technology that goes into lethal autonomous weapons, and some of the difficulties involved in controlling their proliferation.
