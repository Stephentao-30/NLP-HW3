The  Graduate Division’s Guide to Graduate Policy  is the primary source of rules and regulations relating to graduate degrees and programs in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences. This EECS Graduate Student Policies Guide includes rules and regulations that augment the Graduate Division policies. All graduate advisors and graduate students should refer to the  Graduate Division’s Guide to Graduate Policy  when necessary.
