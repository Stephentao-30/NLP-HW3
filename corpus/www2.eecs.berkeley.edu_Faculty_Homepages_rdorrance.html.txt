Biography
Richard Dorrance will be teaching EE 251B in Spring 2025. He received his Ph.D. from UCLA in 2015. He is currently a systems research scientist at Intel Corporation. His areas of interest include machine learning algorithms and hardware for wireless communications, remote and autonomous driving systems, wireless signal processing, and compute-in-memory systems.
